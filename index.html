About Us

MUCISA, Organization

MUCISA stands for Maseno University School of Computing Students Association. Dedicated to providing a platform for students pursuing courses in School of Computing and any related discipline within the Institution. It is established with a vision of uniting students in the field of Technology. MUCISA plays a pivotal role in organizing activities partnering with other groups within school e.g., Google for Developers and Microsoft Learn Students. Which promotes academic excellence, career growth and community development. Hackathons being the major activities.

Our Mission

We strive to develop and support school of computing and informatics students' through state of art Technology, Innovation and Leadership to be internationally competent and competititve.
